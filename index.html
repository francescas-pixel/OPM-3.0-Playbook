2026 Direction

2D 3D

In 2026 we will evolve our on-platform marketing from 2D to 3D.

This means levelling up our OPM campaigns to be more creative and entertaining, and finding more ways to demonstrate that we're fans too.

By investing in deeper platform integration, storytelling & interactivity we add another dimension to our campaigns.

Guiding Principles

Make it Matter

Connect your message to the creators, content & moments that users care about.

Get Creative

Leverage animation, contextual triggers, personalized copy, marketing experiences and video to use OPM to its fullest potential.

Quality > Quantity

For OPM, sometimes less is more when it comes to copy, reach, or campaign formats.

Don't Force It

Aim to add value, not a distraction. Complement other channels, don't duplicate.

Campaign Framework

Great campaigns don't try to achieve everything with a single message, instead they tailor multiple messages and formats to the objectives.

Hero Tactics

Formats that inspire and entertain our users.

A single hero tactic such as a Marketing Experience or Watch Feed drives awareness and interest through interactivity and storytelling.

Impact Tactics

Formats that utilise targeting & personalisation to drive action.

Impact tactics such as personalised & contextual in-app messages capture the demand created through hero tactics and paid + ATL efforts.

Ecosystem Tactics

More subtle and native ways to support the campaign.

Ecosystem tactics such as programming, partner playlists & native promotions create more opportunities for users to organically engage.

Core Formats

Banners

Blend right into the home page with native banners that achieve broad distribution.

  • Machine learning targeting
  • High reach, minimal disruption
  • Desktop & mobile support

Modals

Creative canvas for animation, personalization, and multiple CTAs.

  • High priority campaigns
  • Multiple CTA buttons
  • Creative flexibility

Content Stripes

Discreet, contextual messages that appear at the top of the screen without overlaying content.

  • Short & concise messaging
  • Contextual triggers
  • Minimal disruption

Share to Social

Enable users to share content directly to their social platforms.

  • Viral distribution
  • Custom share assets
  • Multi-platform support
